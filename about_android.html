LesS/KEY for Android

LesS/KEY is a passwort generation and management tool based on S/KEY.

Features:

  • The generated passwords (called 'keys' below) are strong but easy to remember.

  • The keys can be (re)generated at any time and on any device (without having to sync a file).

  • The keys (and your master password) are not stored and not sent anywhere.

How to use it (basic usage)

  1. Enter a name for the account you wish to create a password for into the 'seed' field (e. g. 'amazon' or 'github.com').

  2. Enter your master password into the first 'secret' field (optionally also into the second one, for verification) and tap 'calculate'.

    The generated keys are invisible. Tapping 'show' makes them visible, but that's optional.

  3. Double-tap a key to copy it to the clipboard, or select it first and then tap 'copy'.
